Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account and use Budgeto, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter manually.
  • Usage data — basic information about how you interact with the app, such as pages visited and actions taken, used solely to improve the Service.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide and improve Budgeto. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Display your expense history and spending summaries within the app.
  • Authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve app performance.

We will never use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to delivering the Service to you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, all personal data associated with your account will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

You are responsible for keeping your password secure. We recommend using a strong, unique password and not sharing your credentials with anyone.

4. Third-Party Services

Budgeto does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may use trusted third-party services for infrastructure (such as hosting and database providers). These providers are contractually bound to process your data only as necessary to support the Service and in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

The app loads fonts from Google Fonts. This request is made directly from your browser to Google's servers. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle that data.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and transparently.

You may also request a copy of your personal data or ask us to correct or delete it at any time by contacting us directly.
